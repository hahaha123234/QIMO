Taste of Wuxi

Wuxi cuisine is famous for its "sweet, fresh, and glutinous" flavors, a typical representative of Jiangnan culinary culture. From the thin-skinned, juicy xiaolongbao to the richly sauced Wuxi spare ribs, every dish carries the unique flavor of this city.
