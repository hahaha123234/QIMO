Signature Dishes

Wuxi's Three Treasures

Wuxi cuisine is famous for its "sweet, fresh, and glutinous" flavors. These three dishes are classic must-try foods when visiting Wuxi

Must-Try

Wuxi Xiaolongbao

Wuxi xiaolongbao, also known as "xiaolong mantou", is Wuxi's most representative traditional delicacy. It features thin skin, rich broth, tender meat, and fresh filling. Dipped in fragrant vinegar and ginger, one bite releases the delicious broth in the mouth, leaving a lasting impression. Wuxi xiaolongbao has its own characteristics compared to those in Shanghai and Changzhou, with a sweeter taste and more软糯 texture.

  • Paper-thin skin, translucent to see the filling
  • Rich broth, tender and juicy meat filling
  • Sweet taste, with Jiangnan characteristics
  • Century-old exquisite production techniques

Classic Dish

Wuxi Braised Spare Ribs

Wuxi braised spare ribs, also known as "meat bones", is one of Wuxi's three famous products, created during the Guangxu period of the Qing Dynasty. Using premium pork ribs, slow-cooked with dozens of Chinese medicinal herbs and spices, it has a酱红 color, crispy aroma, and rich flavor. Sweet and salty in balance, the meat is tender and the bone fragrance is rich, making it Wuxi's most proud culinary representative.

  • Uses premium pork ribs with firm texture
  • Secret sauce, fragrant aroma
  • Tender meat that melts in your mouth
  • Balanced sweet and salty, long aftertaste

Lake Delicacies

Taihu Three Whites

Taihu Three Whites refer to white fish, white shrimp, and silver fish, the most famous aquatic specialties of Taihu Lake. Taihu Lake has clear water and abundant aquatic plants, producing fish and shrimp with tender meat and delicious taste. Steamed white fish, salted water white shrimp, and silver fish scrambled eggs are the best ways to taste Taihu flavors, allowing you to experience the freshness and purity of Taihu Lake on your tongue.

  • Taihu white fish - tender meat, fresh and not fishy
  • Taihu white shrimp - thin shell, tender meat, sweet and delicious
  • Taihu silver fish - crystal clear, fresh and smooth texture
  • Best season to taste: Spring and Autumn

More Delicacies

Wuxi Specialty Snacks

In addition to the three famous products, Wuxi has many mouth-watering traditional snacks waiting for you to explore

Clear Water Oil Gluten

Traditional Vegetarian Delicacy

Wuxi oil gluten is a unique traditional food with a golden crispy exterior and a soft, chewy interior. It can be made into soup, braised, or cold dishes, each method having its own unique flavor. Stuffed oil gluten is a classic, combining fresh meat filling with oil gluten, a regular on Wuxi people's dining tables.

Price: ¥20-40 per person All districts

Yulan Cake

Sweetness by the Ancient Canal

Yulan cake is a famous traditional pastry in Wuxi, named for its resemblance to magnolia flowers. The exterior is crispy and golden, with both sweet and savory fillings. Sweet fillings are mostly red bean paste and sesame, while savory fillings include fresh meat and radish shreds. Freshly出锅的 Yulan cake has an enticing aroma, crisp and delicious, one of Wuxi people's favorite breakfast items.

Price: ¥5-15 per person Old town area

Recommended Restaurants

Selected representative restaurants in Wuxi, bringing you the most authentic Wuxi flavors

Wangxingji

Traditional Store · Xiaolongbao

Founded in 1913, it is Wuxi's most famous time-honored xiaolongbao shop. With century-old inherited production techniques, every basket of xiaolongbao maintains the most authentic Wuxi flavor.

Address: Zhongshan Road, Liangxi District, Wuxi Opening hours: 6:30-20:00

Sanfengqiao

Time-honored · Braised Spare Ribs

The birthplace of Wuxi braised spare ribs, founded during the Guangxu period of the Qing Dynasty. The braised spare ribs here have a bright red color, crispy and delicious, the best choice for tasting authentic Wuxi braised spare ribs.

Address: Sanfengqiao, Liangxi District, Wuxi Opening hours: 9:00-21:00

Taihu Renjia

Lake Delicacies · Taihu Three Whites

Located on the shores of Taihu Lake, specializing in fresh Taihu aquatic products. Daily direct supply of white fish, white shrimp, and silver fish from the lake, allowing you to taste the freshest Taihu flavors.

Address: Taihu Avenue, Binhu District, Wuxi Opening hours: 10:30-21:30
